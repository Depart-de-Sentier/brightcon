Conference

Brightcon 2021 will be held on September 15, 2021, from 9:00 to 16:30 CEST. If conditions permit, it will be a hybrid conference, with at least one on-site location at ETHZ in Z√ºrich, Switzerland; online attendance via Zoom. Attendance is free, but please register if you wish to attend in person, as space is limited.

To stay updated on this annual conference, subscribe to our mailing list.

Call for talks

Please submit talk proposals to the Brightcon organization email: brightcon-organization@brightway.groups.io. Your proposal should include your name, and talk title, brief abstract, and session. You can also organize special sessions! The sessions included so far are:

  • Project showcase: Brightway
  • Project showcase: Open open software and data
  • Idea marketplace: Introduce your incomplete idea, or identify areas where open approaches are needed, and find others to work with
  • Release party: Release and show off your open software or data

Party & Hackathon

In addition to the conference, there will be a party celebrating our community on the evening of September 15 at Dorfsteig 8, 5223 Riniken, about 30 minutes from Z√ºrich. We will also have a hackathon on September 16 & 17 at the same location, with food and lodging provided to participants.

Possible hackathon topics include:

  • Implement/finish multifunctionality
  • Support OpenLCA as an alternative database backend
  • Updating Brightway to use the new packaging standard (pyproject.toml, no setup.py)
  • Brightway 3 ontology and database schema

Hackathon participants will be expected to attend an organizational meeting before the hackathon, and prepare enough to make a meaningful contribution.
